# Concepts & Terminology

Short definitions of key concepts used throughout the project:

- **α, β, γ** – Weighting factors for visual, spatial and temporal diversity (α+β+γ=1.0)
- **d_min** – Spatial minimum distance constraint between selected tiles
- **UMAP** – Dimensionality reduction for visualization & clustering
- **DINOv2 / ResNet50** – Feature extractors used for visual embeddings
- **Facility Location** – Submodular selection objective used via `apricot-select`
- **Bootstrap** – Resampling method for uncertainty quantification
